Discover the Beauty of Southern California

Santa Monica Pier: Situated in Los Angeles, the Santa Monica Pier is a famous landmark that has been showcased in various movies and TV shows. For your enjoyment there are carnival games and rides. You can also explore the aquarium or take a leisurely stroll along the pier to take in the beautiful ocean views and maybe sunset. The beach is a great spot for surfing, volleyball, and we highly recommend stopping by our fruit trucks to refuel with fresh and healthy snacks while enjoying your day at the Santa Monica Pier.

Newport Beach: Located in Orange County, Newport Beach is a attractive coastal town with a wide range of attractions: beautiful beaches, trendy shops, restaurants, and a lively marina. It's a known place by the locals as a place to surf, paddleboard, or take a sunset cruise.
