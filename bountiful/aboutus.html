Our History

In the heart of Carlsbad, Bountiful Foods was founded in 1980 by a group of farmers and entrepreneurs that saw an opportunity to create something special: this opportunity united a small-scale growers and producers under a common mission of sustainable, community-based food production. We have been providing high-quality products and services to the community. We are proud to have served generations of families over the years and are committed to continuing that tradition.

Over the years, Bountiful Foods has grown and expanded. We have built relationships with farmers and suppliers across the region and established a status as a leader in sustainable food progress. Today, the company operates multiple retail locations and a thriving wholesale business. Restaurants and markets throughout the area are being supplied with fresh, seasonal products.
