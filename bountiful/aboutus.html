Our History

Bountiful Foods was founded in 1980 in the heart of Carlsbad. Bountiful Foods was created by a group of farmers and entrepreneurs that saw an opportunity to create something special: a cooperative that would unite small-scale growers and producers under a common mission of sustainable, community-based food production. Since then, we have been providing high-quality products and services to the community. We are proud to have served generations of families over the years and are committed to continuing that tradition.

Over the years, Bountiful Foods has grown and expanded, building relationships with farmers and suppliers across the region and establishing a reputation as a leader in the sustainable food movement. Today, the company operates multiple retail locations and a thriving wholesale business, supplying restaurants and markets throughout the area with fresh, seasonal products.
