Rule of Thirds

FortiNet

FortiNet link

The rule of thirds is a fundamental design principle that is widely used in graphic design. The rule of thirds is based on the idea that placing elements off-center and avoiding a symmetrical composition creates a more visually appealing and dynamic page. It helps to create balance, interest, and a sense of movement on the page.

Proximity

FortiNet

FortiNet link

Proximity is the elements that need to be ordered visually with the goal of achieving less confusion on the page. This separates content that is not related to another in order to highlight the absence of a connection between them.
